Catrin's Arrabiata

Ingredients

  • 1 glug of Olive Oil
  • 1 medium sized onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 chilli, seeds in
  • 100g pancetta
  • 1 tin of tinned tomatoes
  • 100 ml white wine
  • Grated parmesan

Method

  1. Fry the onion in the olive oil until translucent. Once done, add the garlic followed by the chili.
  2. Add the pancetta and cook until golden.
  3. Add the tinned tomatoes and rinse the tin out with water, adding this in along with the wine. Bring to a simmer. Season.
  4. Lower to a gentle heat, put on a saucepan lid, and allow to cook through, the longer the better.
  5. Towards the end, add a tablespoon of parmesan and season to taste.
  6. Add some salted pasta water to the pan and mix through.
  7. Serve with pasta covered in olive oil.
