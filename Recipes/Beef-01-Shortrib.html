Slow cooked beef short ribs

with pancetta and mushrooms

Ingredients

  • Beef short ribs (5-6 bones)
  • 1 garlic bulb
  • 1 heaped tbsp tomato pur√©e
  • 250ml red wine
  • 250ml beef stock (chicken will work too)
  • 1 pack of bacon / pancetta lardons
  • 1 punnet of chestnut mushrooms
  • Handful of parsley
  • Olive oil

Method

  1. Prepare the beef by slicing alongside the bone, straight down, and season
  2. Place a roasting tray on the hob until nice and hot
  3. Heat the olive oil, then place the meat bone-side up. Sear
  4. Slice a whole garlic bulb in half, slide in to the roasting pan
  5. Stir in a heaped teaspoon of tomato puree and put it in the middle, cook it out. Add the red wine, bring to the boil, and reduce
  6. Add beef stock up until about an inch beneath the meat and bring to the boil
  7. Cover the roasting tray with tin foil so you braise from the bottom and steam from the top. Put in to the oven for 2.5 hours at about 170-180 degrees
  8. Just before you are due to take the short ribs out (5-10 minutes beforehand), prepare the lardons and slice the mushrooms. Fry the lardons until brown, then add the mushrooms
  9. To make the sauce, press the cooked garlic through a sieve into the cooking juices. Scrape the bottom of the sieve into the sauce. Sieve it all through to finish the sauce. Garnish the short ribs with parsley

Source

Chef: Gordon Ramsay
