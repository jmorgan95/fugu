Duck Cassoulet

Ingredients

  • 2 Tins Cannellini Beans
  • 4 Duck Legs
  • 150g Pancetta
  • 6 Sausages
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Carrot
  • 2 Stalks Celery
  • 1 Head Garlic
  • 4 Sprigs Parsley
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 1L Chicken Stock

Method

  1. Dice the onion, and chop carrots and celery into large chunks
  2. Cook the pancetta in a large pot / dutch oven until browned. Remove & set aside. Season the duck legs with pepper and place skin side down in the empty pan. Cook until well browned (6-8 mins). Flip and cook second side until lightly browned. Remove & set aside with the pancetta. Add sausages to the pan, turning until well browned on all sides. Remove from the pan.
  3. Add the onion to the pot and stir, scraping up the fond from the bottom of the pan. Cook until translucent. Add the carrot, celery, garlic, parsley, bay leaves and stock. Bring to a simmer over high heat. Reduce to low, cover, and cook for around 45 mins
  4. Remove the carrots, celery, parsley, bay leaves, and discard. Add the beans, and then the pancetta & sausage to the pot, stirring to incorporate. Place the duck legs skin side up on top.
  5. Cook uncovered on the stove top over a low heat for 2 hours. Add more water as needed.
  6. Pre-heat oven to 150C. After the cassoulet has finished cooking on the stove top, place in the oven for an additional 45 - 60 mins
