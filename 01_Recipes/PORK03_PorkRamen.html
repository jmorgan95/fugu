Quick Pork Ramen

Ingredients

  • 6 strips of bacon
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 medium piece of ginger
  • 3 spring onions
  • 1 red onion
  • 1 chilli
  • 4 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 4 dashes of fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 1 chicken stock
  • 2 packs fresh noodles

Method

  1. Cook bacon in a pot until crispy; remove and drain, leaving the fat in the pot
  2. Dice the garlic, ginger, onions, and chilli. Fry in the bacon fat until soft
  3. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, fish sauce, miso paste, and sriracha. Heat until bubbling
  4. Add the chicken stock and bring to the boil; simmer for 15 - 20 mins
  5. Cook noodles according to packet instructions
  6. Add the noodles, broth and bacon to a bowl, along with any other toppings
