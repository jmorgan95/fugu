Crispy Thai Cod

with tamarind sauce, rice and green beans

Ingredients

  • 2 cod fillets (or other white fish)
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 red chilli
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 spring onion
  • 4 tbsp cornflour
  • 100g green beans
  • 300g basmati rice
  • 15g tamarind paste
  • 15ml fish sauce
  • 10ml soy sauce
  • 2tsp sugar
  • 1 handful coriander

Method

  1. Add the fish fillets to a plate and drizzle the soy sauce over them. Turn several times until the soy sauce fully soaks into the fish. Sprinkle the cornflour all over, rub into both sides of the fish until coated, and then set aside for later. (Coat the fish in the cornflour one side at a time to avoid stickiness
  2. Peel and finely slice the shallots and garlic. Chop half of the red chilli into rounds, then chop the remaining chilli finely. Roughly chop the coriander, and thinly slice the spring onion
  3. Add basmati rice and 300ml cold water to a pot with a lid and bring to the boil over a high heat. Once boiling, reduce the heat to very low and cook, covered, for an initial 5 mins
  4. Boil the kettle. Heat a large, wide-based pan with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a medium high heat. Once hot, add the coated fish and cook for 8-9 mins, turning once halfway until golden, crisp and cooked through
  5. While the fish is cooking, combine the tamarind paste, fish sauce, 2 tsp sugar and 150ml boiled water
  6. Once the rice has had an initial 5 mins, add the green beans to the pot. Cook, covered, for a further 5-7 mins, or until all the water has absorbed and the rice and beans are cooked. Once done, reomve the pot from the heat and keep covered until serving
  7. Heat a separate pot with a drizzle of vegetable oil over a medium heat. Once hot, add the sliced shallot, garlic and chilli, and cook for 1 min. Add the tamarind stock, bring to the boil and cook for 4-5 mins (or until thickened)
  8. Serve the crispy fish with the rice and green beans to the side. Drizzle with the tamarind sauce, and top with coriander, spring onion and remaning chilli

Source

Chef: Gousto
