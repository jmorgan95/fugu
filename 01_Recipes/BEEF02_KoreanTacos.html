Korean Beef Tacos

with Sriracha Mayo & Pickled Cucumber

Ingredients

  • 250g Beef Mince
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • 1/2 Cucumber
  • 2 tbsp Rice Wine Vinegar
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 4 tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 50g Ginger
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Ketjap Manis
  • 2 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tbsp Sriracha
  • 6 Soft Shell Tacos

Method

  1. Finely dice the onion and slice the pepper into thin strips. Peel and grate the garlic and ginger
  2. Heat a drizzle of oil in a frying pan over high heat. Add the beef mince, breaking up with a spoon as it cooks. Once browned, add the onion and pepper, and continue cooking
  3. Trim the ends from the cucumber and thinly slice. Put the rice vinegar, salt and sugar into a bowl with the cucumber, and toss to coat
  4. Mix 1 tbsp sriracha with the mayonnaise in a small bowl
  5. When the onions and peppers are softened, add the garli and ginger. Stir and cook until fragrant
  6. Stir in the soy sauce, ketjap manis, honey and 1 tbsp sriracha. Bring to a bubble and remove from the heat
  7. Assemble by spreading the mayo onto each taco, spooning over some of the beef mixture and topping with the pickled cucumber
