Roast Lamb Rump

with baked aubergine & pesto

Ingredients

  • 200g Lamb Mince
  • 150g Basmati Rice
  • 2 tbsp Ground Turmeric
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Red Chilli
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 1 pack Lentils
  • Indian Spice Blend (Garam Masala, Curry Powder, Chilli Powder, Ginger Powder, Cumin, Sugar, Bay leaf, Salt)
  • 100g passata / tomato paste
  • 1 chicken stock pot
  • 1 bag Baby Spinach

Method

  1. Cook the rice in a pot of boiling water, adding 1 tbsp of turmeric
  2. Heat a large frying pan on high hear. Cook the lamb mince until browned
  3. Dice the onion, and stir into the pan once the lamb mince is browned. Cook until the onion is softened
  4. Deseed and finely chop the chilli and garlic. Add the pan along with the curry powder and remaining turmeric. Cook for 1 min
  5. Drain and rinse the lentils. Add the passata, 100ml of water and lentils to the pot, along with a pinch of sugar. Simmer until thickened (4-5 mins)
  6. Add the spinach a handful at a time, and cook until wilted and hot.
  7. Serve the curry on top of the rice in bowls, topping with any remaining chilli
