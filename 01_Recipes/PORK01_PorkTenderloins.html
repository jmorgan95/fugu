Pork Tenderloins tacos

with guacamole

Ingredients

  • 2 pork tenderloins (about 500g)
  • 125 ml dry red wine
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Thyme, chopped
  • 2 or 3 avocados
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Coriander, chopped
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/4 tsp tabasco
  • 1 green chilli, diced
  • Tortillas

Method

  1. Stir together wine, vinegar, oil, thyme, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper
  2. Place tenderloin in non-metallic container. Pour wine mixture evenly over the pork. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, turning occasionally
  3. Pit and peel the avocados, place in a bowl and mash coarsely with a fork. Mix in the tomato, garlic, coriander, lime juice, tobasco and chilli pepper. Season.
  4. Pre-heat grill. Remove the tenderloins from the marinade, and pat them dry with kitchen roll. Boil the remaining marinade & reserve
  5. Place the tenderloins on the grill, brushing the meat occasionally with the reserved marinade, turning occasionally to brown evenly. Cook for around 15 - 18 mins
  6. Let rest for 5 mins. Cut on the diagonal into thin slices. Serve with the guacamole & tortillas

Source

Chef: Williams-Sonoma

(Complete Grilling Cookbook)
