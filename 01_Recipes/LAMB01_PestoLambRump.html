Roast Lamb Rump

with baked aubergine & pesto

Ingredients

  • 4 lamb rumps (or boneless leg)
  • 200ml olive oil
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  • 1 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp crushed black peppercorns
  • Juice from 1/2 lemon
  • 25g pine nuts
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 100g basil
  • 150ml olive oil
  • 50g grated parmesan
  • 2 large aubergines
  • 100g rocket

Method

  1. They day before cooking, add the herbs, garlic, peppercorns, lemon juice and olive oil to a blender and blitz until pur√©ed. Pour over the lamb, wrap in cling film and leave to rest in the fridge for 24hrs
  2. To prepare the pesto, toast the pine nutes in a dry pan over a medium heat until golden brown. Blitz the garlic, basil leaves, olive oil and pine nuts. Remove from the food processor, mix through the parmesan and store in an airtight container in the fridge
  3. Pre-heat the oven to 200C
  4. Remove the lamb from the fridge and allow to cut to room temperature. Meanwhile, cut the aubergine in half, slash the cut surface with a sharp knife and season with salt, pepper and olive oil
  5. Bake the aubergines in the oven for 20 mins until soft. Meanwhile, heat a large frying pan over a high heat
  6. Sear the lamb on each side (fat side down first) and place in the oven for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and allow to rest for 10 minutes
  7. Remove the aubergines from the oven and spoon a layer of pesto across the flesh. Return to the oven for 5 mins
  8. Slice the lamb and plate with a slice of aubergine and dressed rocket
