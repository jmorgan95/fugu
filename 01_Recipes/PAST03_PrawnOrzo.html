Garlicky Prawns

with green butter orzo

Ingredients

  • 180g raw peeled prawns
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 20g basil
  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 200g orzo
  • 1 stock cube

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200C and boil a kettle. Peel the garlic and roughly tear the basil, including the stalks. Add the garlic, basil and a large knob of butter to a food processor and pulse until blitzed but not smooth
  2. Dissolve stock cube into 450ml of boiled water
  3. Chop the cherry tomatoes in half
  4. Add the orzo, stock and half the basil butter to an oven-proof dish, and gently stir together
  5. Place the chopped tomatoes evenly on top of the orzo (they should sink under the stock). Season with salt and pepper. Put the dish in the oven for 18-20 mins (or until the stock has been absorbed)
  6. While the orzo is in the oven, cut the lemon in half. Heat a large pan over a medium-high heat. Add the remaining basil butter to the pan and cook for 1 min. Add the king prawns and juice of half the lemon. Cook for 4 - 5 mins
  7. Cut the remaining lemon into wedges. Serve the prawns over the top of the orzo. Tear any remaining basil and season

Source

Chef: Gousto
